## Private Event Inquiries

> Ciao,
>
> Thank you for your interest in hosting your private event at Bar Salotto! We're honored you're considering us to be part of your special occasion.
>
> At Bar Salotto, we specialize in intimate, elevated gatherings with a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're planning a birthday celebration, bridal or baby shower, rehearsal lunch, corporate gathering, or family event, our team works closely with you to create a seamless and memorable experience. Our modern Italian menu, curated beverage selections, and attentive service make for a stress-free and enjoyable event for both hosts and guests.
>
> **Event Availability:**
> Private events are limited to Fridays through Sundays between 11:00 AM and 4:00 PM. Please note that dates and spaces are limited and fill quickly, especially during peak seasons.
>
> **Pricing:**
> Events start at $40 per adult and $18 per child (under 10 years old)
> Pricing typically includes a curated menu package, and we're happy to discuss food and beverage enhancements based on your preferences.
>
> If you would like to move forward with booking or check availability for a specific date, please complete our official event inquiry form at barsalotto.com/events.
>
> Kindly note that event inquiries and bookings are not handled over email. Submitting the form ensures we receive all necessary details to properly review your request and follow up promptly.
>
> We look forward to the opportunity to host your event and create something truly special for you and your guests.
