## Email Categories & Handling

| Category | Template | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Job application | Yes — "not actively hiring" | Keep on file message |
| Private event inquiry | Yes — Ciao opener, full template | Fri–Sun, 11am–4pm only. $40 adult / $18 child (under 10). Direct to form. **Not handled over email.** |
| Group dining (large reservation) | No template — call to confirm | Different from private events; may include weeknights |
| Catering inquiry | No template — handle individually | Confirm items, GF options, lead time |
| Donation request | Yes — redirect to barsalotto.com/donation | First-come, first-served after Jan 1 |
| Vendor / supplier solicitation | Yes — decline, ask to stop | Firm but polite |
| Content creator / influencer | Warm engagement | "Interested in exploring" — ask for dates/details |
| Charity/nonprofit (regulars) | Donation template, but Phil may personalize | Use judgment based on relationship |
| Review (Yelp/Google) | No template — personal, warm | Thank reviewer, mention specific detail they praised |
| Customer inquiry (menu, hours) | No template — answer directly | Warm, concise |

## Private Events
- Availability: Fridays–Sundays, 11:00 AM – 4:00 PM only
- Pricing: $40/adult, $18/child (under 10)
- Booking NOT handled over email — all event communication goes through **Toast POS**
- Once inquiry form is submitted at barsalotto.com/events, Phil follows up via Toast
- Deposit + contract required to hold a date

## Catering
- Orders placed online at barsalotto.com/order-catering
- Pricing, options, and serving sizes: barsalotto.com/catering
- Lead time: orders accepted up to **24 hours prior** to desired pickup time
- Catering inquiries over email → direct to website; answer specific questions, then redirect
